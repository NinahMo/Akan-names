Akan Names

This web application is for determining your Akan Name simply by taking in your birthday credentials and your gender then it calculates the week you were born and the exact day . Afterwhich it generates your Akan Name which you will see after submitting your credentials. Akan Names are from the Ghanian culture in which children are given first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

Below are some Ghanian Names:

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame
